Pulses & Legumes

As a cornerstone of global diets, pulses provide vital nutrition and versatility. We export and import a premium selection of pulses sourced from trusted international agricultural partners. Through strict quality assurance protocols, we guarantee optimal freshness, high nutritional retention, and superior taste, catering to the exacting standards of the food service industry, retailers, and final consumers worldwide.

  • Black Matpe (Urad Dal): A staple in South Asian cuisine, utilized heavily in daily meal preparations. Recognized for its robust, earthy flavor and exceptional protein profile.
  • Lentils: Quick-cooking and nutrient-dense. Available in red, green, and brown varieties, offering flexibility for soups, stews, and modern culinary applications.
  • Pigeon Peas: An essential ingredient across Indian and African culinary traditions, offering a balanced combination of protein, dietary fiber, and essential minerals.
  • Kabuli Chickpeas: Premium, large-caliber, cream-colored chickpeas. Perfect for hummus, robust salads, and savory dishes requiring a firm texture.
  • Yellow & Green Peas: Highly versatile legumes used in everything from hearty soups to side dishes, bringing natural sweetness and dense nutrition.
  • Mung Beans: Celebrated for high antioxidant levels and rapid sprouting capabilities, fundamental in diverse Asian and health-conscious food markets.
  • Faba (Broad) Beans: Offering an impressive protein yield, these large beans add substance and flavor to salads, dips, and traditional hearty meals.

Industrial Coal

We facilitate the global energy supply chain through the responsible trade of industrial-grade coal. Recognizing coal's continuing importance in mass electricity generation and global steel production, our inventory focuses on low-impurity, high-calorific-value coal sourced from established global mining operations, ensuring reliable and efficient combustion.

  • Anthracite Coal: The highest rank of coal, prized for its incredibly high carbon content, low volatile matter, and clean-burning properties suitable for metallurgy and purification.
  • Bituminous & Sub-Bituminous Coal: The workhorses of the global energy sector. Offering dense thermal energy vital for mass power generation and cement manufacturing.
  • Lignite Coal: A younger, softer coal primarily heavily utilized in localized power plant generation specifically engineered to handle higher moisture fuels.
